Projects

Fieldwork that grounds the theory

From Andean field treks to laboratory partnerships, our projects give students and professionals direct experience with Peru's geology.

Ongoing

Annual Field Trek

A multi-day expedition through mountain terrain, mapping rock formations and slope conditions with students and mentors side by side.

New

Laboratory Partnership Program

Joint access agreements with partner labs, giving members hands-on testing experience beyond the classroom.

Community

Community Outreach

Working with local communities near infrastructure and mining sites to share geological risk awareness in plain terms.

Education

Mineralogy Workshops

Recurring hands-on sessions covering mineral identification and rock classification for members at every level.

Research

Ground Conditions Studies

Applied research linking subsurface conditions to structural design decisions on real infrastructure projects.

Outreach

Geoscience Expo Participation

Annual presence at the national geoscience exhibition, presenting member research to the wider public.

Organized within the framework of **Slope Stability 2026**, with the support of **IAEG Peru** and **ABGE**, and under the sponsorship of **AKD International**, this intensive workshop offers a practical and comprehensive introduction to the **Engineering Geological Model (EGM) approach**, based on the IAEG C25 Guidelines. Participants will learn how to apply EGM throughout the entire mining project cycle—from conception to closure.

**Date & Time:**  
 📅 Friday, October 24, 2026  
 🕗 08:00–12:00 / 14:00–18:00  
 📍 Lima Convention Center – Peru

**Instructors:** [Felicia Weir](https://www.linkedin.com/in/felicia-weir-7576868a/) & [Mark Eggers](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mark-eggers-167a0ba4/) (Australia)  
 **Moderator:** Erik Wunder (Brazil)

Enhance your professional expertise and actively contribute to the international exchange of knowledge in engineering geology.

**Special Benefit:** Members of IAEG Latin American national groups are eligible for discounted registration forming groups of 10 and more. For full details, please contact us at: iaegperu@gmail.com.

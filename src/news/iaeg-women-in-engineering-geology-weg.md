Introducing IAEG Women in Engineering Geology (WEG)✨

We are proud to present the Women in Engineering Geology and the Environment (WEG) Committee of the International Association for Engineering Geology and the Environment (IAEG).

WEG was created to strengthen the visibility, participation, and leadership of women in women in geosciences, engineering and the environment. WEG mission is to promote equity, diversity, and inclusion (EDI). With members from around the world, WEG is a growing network where women in our fields share experiences, build partnerships, and work together to inspire the next generation. Join us in advancing equity and shaping the future of engineering geology!

Learn more: <https://iaeg.info/weg/>

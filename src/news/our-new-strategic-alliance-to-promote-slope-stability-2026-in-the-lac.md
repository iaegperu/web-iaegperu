We are proud to announce a new strategic alliance with the [**Brazilian Association of Engineering and Environmental Geology (ABGE)**](https://www.abge.org.br/home) and the [**LOP – Large Open Pit Project**](https://www.lopproject.com/) Peruvian Group. This collaboration aims to strengthen the regional participation and outreach of the upcoming [**Slope Stability 2026**](https://www.slopestability2026.com/en) international conference, which will be held in **Lima, Peru**.

Click [here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1odsfudeyWbwmhi54bfkVgI4NoDKTIS2U/view?usp=drive_link) to read the agreement and its objectives (link to a document in Spanish).

Through this partnership, we seek to:

* Foster technical cooperation across Latin America and the Caribbean
* Showcase the geotechnical knowledge generated in the region
* Facilitate broader regional participation in one of the most important global events on slope stability and geotechnical innovation

This is a unique opportunity to project our region’s expertise onto the international stage — from Latin America to the world.

Stay tuned! We will soon be sharing details on how to submit your technical abstracts and become part of this major event.

On March 15, 2025, IAEG Peru hosted the webinar **“Peruvian Women in Geosciences”** as part of its special programme commemorating **International Women’s Day**.

The session brought together a diverse group of women in geosciences, featuring contributions from **Camila Castro, Katia Vila, Lucia Lamadrid, and Alexandra Benites Cañote**, with moderation by **Alina Bedoya Campoverde**. The event also ensured accessibility through sign language interpretation by **Alexandra Aquima**.

The discussion created a space to share experiences, reflect on professional pathways, and highlight the contributions of women in geosciences in Peru. It also fostered dialogue around challenges, opportunities, and the importance of building a more inclusive and collaborative professional community.

This initiative reflects IAEG Peru’s ongoing commitment to promoting diversity, inclusion, and visibility within the geoscience field.

📅 **Date:** March 15, 2025  
💻 **Format:** Live online session (Facebook Live)  
💬 **Hashtag:** #YoMujerEnGeociencias

**A national platform for advancing engineering geology and geotechnics in Peru**

The **Peruvian Geotechnical Biennial** is a national initiative led by the **Peruvian National Group of the International Association for Engineering Geology and the Environment (IAEG Peru)**. It is held **every two years** and aims to promote geotechnical knowledge, collaboration, and innovation to support sustainable development and disaster risk reduction in Peru and across Latin America.

The Biennial is open to **students, professionals, academics, and institutions** interested in geotechnical practice and education. It serves as a dynamic space for sharing cutting-edge developments, fostering collaboration between sectors, and building technical capacity in geotechnics, engineering geology, and related fields.

### 🎯 Purpose and Vision

The Biennial seeks to:

* Promote best practices in geotechnical and engineering geology.
* Provide a platform for dialogue between academia, government, and industry.
* Showcase national and regional advances in risk reduction and sustainable development.
* Support inclusive participation, especially of young professionals and underrepresented groups.

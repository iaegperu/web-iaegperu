The Peruvian National Group of the International Association for Engineering Geology and the Environment (IAEG Peru) is inviting applications for the following volunteer positions:

**Available roles**

* **Webmaster**
* **General Secretary**

These roles offer a unique opportunity to contribute to the growth of our professional community, strengthen international collaboration, and support initiatives in engineering geology and related fields.

📩 Interested candidates are invited to submit their application and short proposal to: **iaegperu@gmail.com**

⏰ **Deadline for applications: September 15, 2025**

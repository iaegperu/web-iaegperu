The IAEG Women in Engineering Geology (WEG) Committee is developing a global list of female keynote speakers and invited lecturers to be published on the IAEG website. This initiative will support conference organizers in identifying outstanding women in our field and ensure greater visibility of female experts worldwide. By joining the list, you will:

🔹 Highlight your professional achievements

🔹 Be considered for keynote and invited lectures internationally

🔹 Contribute to advancing diversity and representation in geoscience

📌 We warmly invite all colleagues who wish to be part of this initiative to share their details through [the following form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScubY41KdFto0qbfP2wmACVb1gozY7fzhFOJsiWDYnaYYn1lw/viewform).

For more information or questions, please visit the IAEG WEG Page. Together, let’s build a stronger and more visible future for women in engineering geology.

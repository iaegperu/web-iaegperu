We invite our members and the broader mining and geoscience community to submit their research to the Journal of Advanced Mining Modeling (JAMM), a peer-reviewed scientific journal published by the National University of Trujillo (Peru).  
JAMMs’ Scope includes:  
• Mining engineering  
• Advanced mining systems modeling  
• Artificial intelligence applications across the mining value chain  
  
This is an excellent opportunity to disseminate original research and contribute to the advancement of innovation in mining and engineering geology.  
📲 Submission guidelines

When submitting by email, the corresponding author **must include** the following documents:

* **Manuscript (Word format)**, without author names or affiliations (for double-blind review)
* **Signed Conflict of Interest Declaration**
* **List of authors and contributions** (Excel format)
* **Graphical Abstract** (PNG, JPG, and SVG formats)

For more details, please read the full guidelines on [the JAMM website](https://revistas.unitru.edu.pe/index.php/jamm/about/).

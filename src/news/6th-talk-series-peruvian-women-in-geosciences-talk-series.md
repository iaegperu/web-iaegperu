### From [WEG Peru](/portfolio-our-committees/weg), we are pleased to invite you to a new edition of our talk series

This is a space designed to highlight, inspire, and connect women who are transforming engineering geology, environmental engineering, and the geosciences in Peru.

This session will feature outstanding professionals who will share their experiences across different fields, along with a special international guest:  
**Marisol Valerio – Senior Principal Engineer (WSP) & President of Women in Mine Stability (WIMS)**

📅 March 21  
🕕 06:00 p.m. (Peru time)  
💻 Online (Zoom)

This talk aims to create a space for dialogue and reflection on the challenges, opportunities, and the role of women in our profession, fostering a more inclusive and collaborative community.

✨ We look forward to welcoming you as we continue building the future of engineering geology, environmental engineering, and geosciences in Peru and beyond.

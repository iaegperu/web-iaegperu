We are pleased to announce that preparations for the **IV Peruvian Geoscience Olympiad** are now underway.

We are currently reviewing the competition guidelines in collaboration with **CIGECC** and the **IUGS Commission on Geoscience Education (COGE)** to further strengthen the educational quality and international alignment of the Olympiad.

As we begin this new edition, we invite enthusiastic geoscientists, researchers, university lecturers, graduate students, and industry professionals to join us as **volunteer mentors and trainers**. Your expertise can help inspire and prepare the next generation of Peruvian students for the **International Earth Science Olympiad (IESO)** while promoting excellence in Earth science education across the country.

More information about the official call, timeline, and mentoring opportunities will be announced soon.

Together, let's inspire the next generation of geoscientists!

We are pleased to officially introduce **Eng.** [**Sandra Melisa Ramírez**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sandramelisaramirezarias7777777/), President of the Engineering Geology Chapter – *Colegio de Ingenieros del Perú (CIP Lima)*, as the **General Coordinator of the 2nd Peruvian Biennial in Geotechnics**.

Eng. Sandra Melisa Ramírez is a geologist from the San Marcos National University of Peru, with over 20 years of experience in the mining sector. She has a strong background in environmental management, community relations, and socio-environmental conflict resolution. Her professional profile is complemented by an international Master’s degree in Environmental Sciences and further specialisations in water resources and interculturality.

Her leadership represents a strategic bridge between the professional, academic, and institutional sectors, strengthening the technical foundation and national projection of the Biennial.

📍 **2nd Peruvian Biennial in Geotechnics**  
📅 September 2–4, 2026  
📌 Lima, Peru

We continue moving forward towards an event that will consolidate Peruvian geotechnics as a regional benchmark.

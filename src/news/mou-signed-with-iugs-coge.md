The recent signature of the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with IUGS-COGE means more support to our effort to improve the quality of geoscience content in the Peruvian Curriculum.

Through the collaboration with IUGS-COGE we are supporting regional capacity building, professional development, and the integration of engineering geology perspectives into geoscience education and outreach initiatives in Latin America with emphasis in Peru.

Read the Memoramdum of Understanding suscribed with COGE [here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1H02xPRHU-8OL8U0UqoTIf9sFBQzGCpJJ/view?usp=drive_link).

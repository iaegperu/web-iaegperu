We are proud to announce that **IAEG’s Women in Engineering Geosciences (WEG)** has signed a **Memorandum of Understanding (MoU)** with **Women in Mining International (WIMs)**.

This alliance strengthens global efforts to promote:  
 ✔️ Gender equity  
 ✔️ Leadership opportunities  
 ✔️ Professional growth for women in geosciences & mining

**Read here the** [**MoU**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1ftUjdUDlosCMGHD_SVy_MJKN2Qu5bKIt/view?usp=drive_link) **for full context.**

From **IAEG Peru**, we celebrate this milestone and reaffirm our commitment to building bridges between the mining and geosciences communities worldwide.

Since 2021, our community has been organizing the **Annual Forums “Peruvian Women in Geosciences”** firts with the support of **IAPG Peru** as a local initiative and now this evolved into a flagship activity of **WEG Peru**, where we bring together voices, experiences, and perspectives of women across the geosciences.

### 🎯 Our Purpose

* Give visibility to the contributions of Peruvian women in geosciences.
* Foster intergenerational dialogue and mentorship.
* Inspire and empowering young students and early-career professionals to pursue geoscience pathways.
* Promote equity, diversity, and inclusion across the geosciences, engineering and related fields

### 🌍 Growing Together

From now on, these annual events will be organized by **WEG Peru** as part of our commitment to equity and inclusion in geosciences, in collaboration with global networks such as **WIMS, IAEG–COGE**, and other partner organizations.

👉 Stay tuned for upcoming editions and join us in building a more inclusive geoscience community.

**Dear IAEG Peru Members,**

We are pleased to officially present our **2026 Executive Committee**, the team that will assume leadership of our network during the current year:

* **Dr. MSc. Eng. Sandra Villacorta** – President
* **Eng. Rubén Romero** – Vice President
* **BSc. Alessandra Villacorta** – Social Media and Webmaster Coordinator
* **BSc. Hugo Pullch** – YEG Representative
* **Eng. Claudia Sánchez** – WEG Representative
* **Eng. Alonso Otiniano Otiniano** – Coordinator of the 4th Peruvian Geosciences Olympiad
* **Eng. Christian Ayala** – Coordinator of the Disaster Risk Management Working Group

This team will work collaboratively to strengthen the promotion of geosciences applied to engineering, environmental management, the training of young professionals, and the international outreach of the Peruvian Group. The work plan and projects scheduled for this year can be consulted [HERE](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qBbsdvXYld3oOdN3kpzKefy-KA6xolqQ28N-zLVAnnA/edit?gid=0#gid=0).

We thank you all for your continued commitment to IAEG Peru and encourage you to remain actively involved in the network’s initiatives.

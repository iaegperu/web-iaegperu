From the first edtion in 2021 the aim is training high school students and selects Peru's representatives for the International Earth Science Olympiad. The Olympiad promotes Earth literacy, talent development, and equitable access to science education for high school students across Peru. The event is part of IAEG Peru’s commitment to **education, outreach, and inclusion**, the Olympiad identifies and trains top-performing students to represent Peru at international events such as the **International Earth Science Olympiad (IESO)**. It also supports teachers and schools in remote and underserved regions through tailored capacity-building activities. ### Mission and Goals * Promote  **interest in geosciences**  among Peruvian youth. * Strengthen  **school-based geoscience education**  and teacher training. * Identify and prepare national representatives for the **IESO** * Encourage participation of students from  * **underrepresented and rural areas** . * Foster values of  **geoethics, sustainability, and scientific cooperation** .

Committees

The working groups behind IAEG Peru

Each committee focuses on a specific part of our mission — from education to technical standards — led by members who volunteer their time and expertise.

CM 01

Education & Outreach Committee

Organizes workshops, field treks, and public engagement activities — including our presence at the Geoscience Expo — to bring engineering geology to students and the wider community.

CM 02

Technical Standards Committee

Reviews and aligns local practice with international IAEG guidelines, supporting members on ground investigation and reporting standards.

CM 03

Research & Fellowship Committee

Coordinates the Graduate Fellowship Awards and supports member research through laboratory partnerships and mentorship.
